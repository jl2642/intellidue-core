# Production Product Standard

## Product stack

| Layer | Product | User purpose | Minimum gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| L0 | Source Registry / Intake | establish source identity and scope | deterministic inventory and disposition |
| L1 | Professional Workpaper | testing, recalculation and exceptions | inputs, procedure, result, exception, conclusion, source tie-out |
| L2 | Specialist Reports | domain findings and limits | performed/unperformed procedures and reviewer boundary |
| L3 | Integrated DD Report | cross-domain decision issues | issue-led synthesis, causal chain, downside and transaction impact |
| L4 | IC Memo | management decision | explicit ask, alternatives, authority, budget, expiry and stop triggers |

## Quality threshold

A Reader Pack is Production-ready only when product-class truthfulness, source transparency, evidence discipline, Reader/Control separation, publication quality, package integrity and portability all pass. Aggregate scores cannot override a failed hard gate.

## Final Full DD upgrade

Final Full DD is an evidence-driven upgrade, not a writing upgrade. It requires material project evidence closure, complete professional procedures, external confirmations or fieldwork when applicable, and named professional sign-off.
