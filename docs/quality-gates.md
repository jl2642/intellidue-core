# Production Quality Gates v1.0

1. Product class is accurate and prominently disclosed.
2. Source registration, depth review, direct tie-out and public research are separately counted.
3. Workpapers record procedure, sample/input, result, exception, conclusion and source.
4. Specialist reports disclose performed and unperformed procedures.
5. Integrated reports are issue-led and include causal links and residual risks.
6. IC memos state requested authority, budget, accountable roles, expiry, reporting and vote.
7. Reader language contains no internal process identifiers.
8. DOCX/PDF/XLSX technical QA passes.
9. Package pointers and hashes are deterministic.
10. Clean-room recovery passes without history or external project files.
11. Human sign-off is required for formal professional reliance.
12. Final Full DD and investment approval remain evidence-gated.
